Additional Saturn Fact

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest in our solar system. It is a gas giant composed mainly of hydrogen and helium, surrounded by stunning rings made of ice and rock particles. Saturn has strong winds, yellowish cloud bands, and more than 80 moons, including Titan, the largest and most Earth-like in terms of atmospheric composition.
