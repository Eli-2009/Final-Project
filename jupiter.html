Additional Jupiter Fact

Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun and the largest in our solar system. It is a gas giant composed mainly of hydrogen and helium, with no solid surface. Jupiter is known for its Great Red Spot, a massive storm larger than Earth. Jupiter has a strong magnetic field and dozens of moons, including the four largest: Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto.
