URANUS

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun, and the third largest planet in our solar system. It appears to spin sideways.

Additional Uranus Fact

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and the third-largest in our solar system. It is an ice giant composed mainly of hydrogen, helium, and methane, giving it a pale blue-green color. A day on Uranus lasts about 17 hours and 14 minutes, while a year takes 84 Earth years. The planet has a faint ring system and at least 27 known moons, with Titania being the largest.
