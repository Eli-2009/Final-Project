Additional Neptune Fact

Neptune is the eighth and farthest planet from the Sun in our solar system. It is an ice giant composed mainly of hydrogen, helium, and methane, giving it a vivid blue color. Neptune has the strongest winds among the planets, reaching speeds of up to 2,100 km/h. A day on Neptune lasts about 16 hours, while a year takes 165 Earth years.
