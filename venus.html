VENUS

Venus is the second planet from the Sun, and the sixth largest planet. It is the hottest planet in our solar system.

Additional Venus Fact

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and the hottest in our solar system. It is similar in size to Earth but has a thick, toxic atmosphere that traps heat, creating a runaway greenhouse effect. Venus has a slow rotation, taking 243 Earth days to complete one spin, making its day longer than its year, which lasts 225 Earth days.
