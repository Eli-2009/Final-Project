MARS

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun, and the seventh largest planet.

Additional Mars Fact

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is often called the "Red Planet" due to its iron-rich, rust-colored surface. It has a thin atmosphere composed mostly of carbon dioxide. With a surface marked by vast deserts, mountains, and the largest volcano in the solar system, Mars experiences extreme temperature variations.
